# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA/Testing Lead

### Role Summary
QA and Testing Leads ensure quality standards are met throughout the development lifecycle. They design test strategies, oversee test execution, validate acceptance criteria, and advocate for quality in release decisions.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain test plans and test cases aligned with acceptance criteria
- Lead manual and automated testing efforts
- Coordinate with developers on test coverage and CI/CD integration
- Validate that acceptance criteria are met before marking work complete
- Identify and triage bugs, tracking severity and resolution
- Conduct smoke tests and regression testing before releases
- Report quality metrics and recommend go/no-go decisions

### Goals
- Deliver high-quality releases with minimal post-release defects
- Enable fast feedback loops through automated testing
- Build confidence in release readiness through comprehensive test coverage

### Typical Communication
- Sprint planning and daily standups
- Test plan reviews with developers and PM
- Quality reports in weekly status updates
- Release readiness attestation

---

## Technical Architect / Tech Lead

### Role Summary
Technical Architects and Tech Leads provide technical direction, design guidance, and ensure architectural decisions support long-term scalability and maintainability.

### Responsibilities
- Define technical architecture and design patterns for new initiatives
- Conduct design reviews and guide implementation decisions
- Identify and propose solutions for technical debt and scalability risks
- Mentor developers on best practices and code quality standards
- Coordinate integration points and cross-system dependencies
- Advise on technology choices and trade-offs
- Ensure technical decisions align with company standards and security requirements

### Goals
- Build systems that are maintainable, scalable, and secure
- Reduce technical risk through thoughtful design and early review
- Accelerate team productivity through clear technical guidance

### Typical Communication
- Technical design reviews and architecture discussions
- Code reviews and implementation guidance
- Planning sessions to assess technical feasibility
- Documentation of key technical decisions

---

## Scrum Master / Agile Coach

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate process consistency, remove blockers, and coach teams on agile practices. They are servant-leaders focused on team effectiveness and process improvement.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate sprint planning, daily standups, and retrospectives
- Help the team maintain Definition of Done and process discipline
- Identify and escalate blockers that impede progress
- Protect the team from mid-sprint scope changes
- Coach the team on agile best practices
- Track velocity and highlight process improvements
- Coordinate with PM on backlog refinement and dependency management

### Goals
- Enable predictable, sustainable team velocity
- Foster a culture of continuous improvement and psychological safety
- Reduce cycle time and unplanned work through process discipline

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint ceremonies
- Retrospective action item tracking
- Escalations for blockers and dependencies
- Velocity and burndown visibility

---

## Sponsor / Stakeholder Lead

### Role Summary
Sponsors and Stakeholder Leads represent business priorities, authorize work, and ensure projects deliver on business objectives. They provide strategic direction and make critical trade-off decisions.

### Responsibilities
- Define business objectives and success metrics for initiatives
- Provide strategic prioritization and approval for scope changes
- Engage cross-functional stakeholders and communicate vision
- Review and approve release go/no-go decisions
- Escalate and resolve business-level risks and dependencies
- Advocate for team needs and secure necessary resources
- Participate in milestone reviews and retrospectives

### Goals
- Ensure projects deliver measurable business value
- Remove organizational blockers that impede delivery
- Align project outcomes with company strategy and customer needs

### Typical Communication
- Monthly stakeholder updates and milestone reviews
- Executive escalations for critical decisions or risks
- Release announcements and impact communication
- Strategic roadmap alignment sessions
